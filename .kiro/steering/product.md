# Product Overview

Grapadi Company Profile is a public-facing company website for Grapadi, a digital/software services firm. It serves as both a marketing site and a lightweight CRM/project management tool for internal use.

## Public Site

- Homepage with hero section, client logos, services overview, and CTA
- About page with executive team and company information
- Timeline page showing company milestones
- Services listing with individual service detail pages
- Portfolio showcase
- Blog with article detail pages
- Contact page with inquiry form
- Newsletter subscription

## Admin Panel (Filament)

Accessible at `/gp-strategix`, the admin panel manages:

- Articles (blog/content)
- Services and service categories
- Portfolio items and portfolio categories
- Projects (with contract and report PDF generation)
- Leads and interactions (basic CRM)
- Executive team profiles
- Company timeline entries
- Clients and brands
- FAQs
- Site settings
- User management with role-based permissions (Filament Shield)

## SEO & Marketing

- Dynamic XML sitemap generation
- robots.txt route
- Newsletter subscribe/unsubscribe functionality
- Lead capture modal (Livewire)
